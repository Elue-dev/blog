Cooking Meat Properly

It is very important to cook meat properly to get rid of some infectious agent situated within the meat. Some parasites during their development have a form in the muscle of animals called bradyzoite, this is then ingested by humans when they consume animal meat and so get infected in the process. Cooking the meat properly can prevent this occurence, this is because, there is a particular temperature range at which these infectious agents can survive when subjected to heat, so proper cooking can kill them, and the meat is rendered harmless. Tell your mum, tell your cooks, cook your meat properly. Health is wealth. Stay healthy.
