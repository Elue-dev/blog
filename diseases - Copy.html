Diseases associated with meat.

There are several diseases that are directly related to all the domestic meat species of beef, pork, lamb, and poultry, These include:

. Bovine Spongiform Encephalitis from beef cattle.

. Scarpie from lamb and mutton.

. Echerichia coli from ground beef.

. Salmonella from poultry

. Trichinosis from pork

There are 2 aspects of these associated disease, it could be: Foodborne Infection: Which is caused by ingesting food that has been contaminated by bacteria, parasites and viruses. Examples are Salmonella and Trichinosis Foodborne Intoxication: Which is caused when food has been contaminated with toxic chemicals either the ones produced by the bacteria (talked about in the previous write up) such as Echerichia coli or chemicals from Pesticides etc. These diseases can causes serious ill-health, and could proove fatal sometimes, so be careful of meat and meat products.
Wash and Cook your meat properly. Why is it important to cook meat properly?? Some of these pathogenic organisms have a particular temperature range at which they can survive, and so the heat from cooking produces a temperature which the organisms cannot cope with or withstand, and so they die in the process. So never eat an undercooked meat, cook properly. I hope this information helps. Health is Wealth. Stay Healthy.
